# Marketing Hero Assets

The homepage hero uses supplied rendered PNG scenes as light and dark backgrounds:

- `public/marketing/hero-background-light.png`
- `public/marketing/hero-background-dark.png`

The rendered backgrounds provide the desk, monitor, map, lamp, mug, plant, and other environmental artwork. The app layer owns the navigation, copy, CTAs, liquid-glass signal panel, ticker, and surrounding page color system.
